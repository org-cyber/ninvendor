How It Works

01

Enter Your NIN

Input your 11-digit National Identification Number into the lookup field above. Your NIN was assigned to you at the point of enrolment at any NIMC centre.

02

Record Is Retrieved

Your identity details are securely fetched from the NIMC central database, including your biographic data and registered photograph.

03

Print or Save

Your digital NIN slip is generated instantly. Use the Print button to produce a physical copy or save a PDF — accepted wherever a NIN slip is required.
